high (0x00000002) – High complexity / low speed encoding

on (0x00000000) – Deblocking on

off (0x00000001) – Deblocking off

not-slice-boundaries (0x00000002) – Deblocking on, except for slice boundaries

RC_MODES (not introspectable)

quality (0x00000000) – Quality mode

bitrate (0x00000001) – Bitrate mode

buffer (0x00000002) – No bitrate control, just using buffer status

off (0xffffffff) – Rate control off mode

auto (0x00000005) – Number of slices equal to number of threads

camera (0x00000000) – video from camera

screen (0x00000001) – screen content
