GstGLRenderBuffer

GstGLRenderbuffer is a GstGL.GLBaseMemory subclass providing support for OpenGL renderbuffers.

GstGL.GLRenderbuffer is created or wrapped through GstGL.prototype.gl_base_memory_alloc with GstGL.GLRenderbufferAllocationParams.
