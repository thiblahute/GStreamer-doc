Enumerations

GstMpeg2encAspect

0 (0x00000000) – Deduce from input

1 (0x00000001) – 1:1

2 (0x00000002) – 4:3

3 (0x00000003) – 16:9

4 (0x00000004) – 2.21:1

GstMpeg2encFormat

0 (0x00000000) – Generic MPEG-1

1 (0x00000001) – Standard VCD

2 (0x00000002) – User VCD

3 (0x00000003) – Generic MPEG-2

4 (0x00000004) – Standard SVCD

5 (0x00000005) – User SVCD

6 (0x00000006) – VCD Stills sequences

7 (0x00000007) – SVCD Stills sequences

8 (0x00000008) – DVD MPEG-2 for dvdauthor

9 (0x00000009) – DVD MPEG-2

10 (0x0000000a) – ATSC 480i

11 (0x0000000b) – ATSC 480p

12 (0x0000000c) – ATSC 720p

13 (0x0000000d) – ATSC 1080i

GstMpeg2encFramerate

0 (0x00000000) – Same as input

1 (0x00000001) – 24/1.001 (NTSC 3:2 pulldown converted film)

2 (0x00000002) – 24 (native film)

3 (0x00000003) – 25 (PAL/SECAM video)

4 (0x00000004) – 30/1.001 (NTSC video)

5 (0x00000005) – 30

6 (0x00000006) – 50 (PAL/SECAM fields)

7 (0x00000007) – 60/1.001 (NTSC fields)

8 (0x00000008) – 60

GstMpeg2encInterlaceMode

-1 (0xffffffff) – Format default mode

0 (0x00000000) – Progressive

1 (0x00000001) – Interlaced, per-frame encoding

2 (0x00000002) – Interlaced, per-field-encoding

GstMpeg2encVideoNorm

0 (0x00000000) – Unspecified

p (0x00000070) – PAL

n (0x0000006e) – NTSC

s (0x00000073) – SECAM

GstMpeg2encPlaybackFieldOrders

0 (0xffffffff) – Unspecified

1 (0x00000001) – Top-field first

2 (0x00000002) – Bottom-field first

GstMpeg2encQuantisationMatrix

9 (0x00000000) – Default

1 (0x00000001) – High resolution

2 (0x00000002) – KVCD

3 (0x00000003) – TMPGEnc
