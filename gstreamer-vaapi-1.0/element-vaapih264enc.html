element vaapih264enc

Encodes raw video streams into H.264 bitstreams.

Example launch line |[ gst-launch-1.0 -ev videotestsrc num-buffers=60 ! timeoverlay ! vaapih264enc ! h264parse ! mp4mux ! filesink location=test.mp4 ]|
