element vaapimpeg2enc

Encodes raw video streams into MPEG2 bitstreams.

Example launch line |[ gst-launch-1.0 -ev videotestsrc num-buffers=60 ! timeoverlay ! vaapimpeg2enc ! matroskamux ! filesink location=test.mkv ]|
